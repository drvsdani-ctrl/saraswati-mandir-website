## 2026-07-17

### Standing rules learned this session
- Activity/page names in nav arrows (pagenav "previous/next" links) and footer links must use the **full official name**, not a short form.
  - School: `कल्याण मूकबधिर विद्यालय व कनिष्ठ महाविद्यालय` / `Kalyan Mook-Badhir Vidyalaya and Junior College`
  - Library: `कै. सौ. विद्यावती जोगळेकर वाचनालय` / `Late Vidyawati Joglekar Library`
  - Hostel: `कर्मचारी महिला वसतिगृह` / `Working Women's Hostel`
  - Balwadi: `खंडोबा हनुमान बालवाडी` / `Khandoba Hanuman Balwadi`
- Do not bold personal names in body text (founders, presidents, coaches, donors). Keep them plain.
- On the Marathi (`mr`) version, do not append the English term in brackets (e.g. no `अभ्यासिका (Reading Room)` on the mr side). The English term belongs only on the `en` side.
- Do not use decorative colored/emoji placeholder blocks in place of a missing photo. If there is no photo, show text only.
